Tips for Killer UGC Ad Scripts

Get the most out of our AI-Powered Product Script Generator with these simple, actionable tips!

Pick the Perfect Script Length

Want your user-generated content (UGC) to shine? Match the script length to your platform. For snappy social media posts like TikTok or Instagram Reels, choose Short (~120 words). It’s ideal for quick, punchy content that grabs attention fast. For product videos—whether you’re using an avatar or filming yourself—go with Medium (~250 words) for a bit more detail or Long (~500 words) for in-depth explainers. Picking the right size makes your message pop where it matters most!

Refresh Until It’s Just Right

Not vibing with the first script? No problem! Hit "Generate Script" again. Our AI-powered product script generator rotates through styles—casual, friendly, bold, and more—so you get a fresh take every time. Keep clicking until the script feels perfect for your voice or audience. It’s like having a brainstorming buddy that never runs out of ideas!

Keep Your Details Simple

Don’t overthink your input—just paste what you’ve got! Add the product name, price, a few features, or a deal, and let our automated ad script creator turn it into a smooth, sellable script. No need for fancy formatting or long descriptions—the tool does the hard work for you. Simple details in, killer scripts out!

Test and Tweak for Better Results

Make your scripts even better by playing around! Try different lengths or details to see what clicks with your audience. Short on time? Start with a Short script and build up later. Want a specific tone? Toss in a note like "funny" or "serious" with your details—the AI ad script generator will adapt. It’s all about fast, fun, and effective content creation!
